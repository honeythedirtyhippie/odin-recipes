Mashed Potatoes

Description

Is there anything better than some well prepared mashed potatoes?

Ingredients

  • 4lb russet potatoes
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 12tbsp unsalted butter
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and cube potatoes, rince in large pot until water runs clear
  2. Fill pot with water to cover potatoes, season generously with salt
  3. Bring to a boil over med-high heat, reduce and simmer for about 15 mins (or til tender)
  4. Drain, and rinse with hot water, allow to steam in collander to remove moisture
  5. Mash/mix/process potatoes to preffered consistency
  6. Add butter and fold into potatoes
  7. Mound potatoes in centre of pot over medium heat, add milk around centre
  8. Bring milk to simmer before folding into potatoes
  9. Remove from heat, season, and serve.
