Pork Chops

Description

An undeniable classic! Comfort food at it's finest. Serve with country gravy and mashed potatoes, with some cooked greens on the side for the untimate comfort dinner.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1-1/2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp fresh ground pepper 1 tsp salt
  • 1-1/2 tsp cayenne
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce canola oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, paprika, pepper, salt, and cayenne together in a shallow plate.
  2. Beat eggs and hot sauce together in a small bowl, transfer to a shallow plate.
  3. Lightly cover a pork chop in plastic wrap and bash it with the flat side of a meat hammer (from the middle outward) until it is half the thickness it was when you began.
  4. Repeat with other pork chops.
  5. Add enough oil in a heavy high sided pan (like a Dutch oven) to reach half way up the pork chop.
  6. Dredge each pork chop in flour, egg, then flour again and place on a rack. Allow to dry for about 5 minutes while your oil is heating.
  7. Pre-heat oil until it reaches 360 F.
  8. Carefully slip one pork chop into the oil and set a timer for 5 minutes. Ideally, you want the temperature to stay between 360-375 F so keep a close eye on it.
  9. When the timer beeps, carefully flip the pork chop over and fry for another 5 minutes. Remove and place on a paper towel covered plate.
  10. Repeat with the rest of the pork chops, adjusting the temperature as needed.
  11. Enjoy with more hot sauce.
